The Battle of Lexington and Concord

Engraved by Ralph Earl and Amos Doolittle

The image diplayed was created by two miltiamen named Ralph Earl and Amos Doolittle. They did not see the battle themselves but they visited the site a few weeks later and talked to people who did see it. With that information they created a sketch of the battle. Doolittle took the sketch and engraved it on a printing plate and he published his engraving in Deccember 1775
